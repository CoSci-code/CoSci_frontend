CoSci

CoSci aims to build a decentralized hub for scientist profiles, article publishing and preprints, and reviews of articles and scientists.

  • It seeks to standardize research
  • ensure fair evaluation
  • and optimize resource allocation

CoSci is composed of three main products: ArchCredit, ArxPrint, and Sholagram.

  • ArchCredit is a Decentralized Academic Reputation System. It also includes a paper evaluation and rating system, article-length commentaries on papers, and a scientist research competency rating system.
  • ArxPrint is a Web3-based preprint system for academic papers, similar to ArXiv. It supports all-disciplines journals, backed by the research community.
  • Sholagram is a scientist profile and social diagram. It features the integration of existing scientist profiling and social systems, as well as the integration of Web3 wallet systems and related frameworks.
